About Us

The North South University Public Health And Sciences Club is a student-led organization that aims to promote public health and sciences awareness among students and the community.

Our Mission

  • To raise awareness about public health and sciences issues
  • To provide opportunities for students to get involved in public health and sciences activities
  • To connect students with public health and sciences professionals and experts
